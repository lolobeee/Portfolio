3. Setup Instructions

3.1 Turning on your Steam Deck for the first time

  1. Press the power button
  2. When prompted, select your preferred language from the options listed on the screen:
  1. Select your Wi-Fi Network from the list provided
  2. Login with your network credentials

Note:

  • If the Steam Deck is unable to connect to your network, double check your credentials and/or internet connection and try again.

3.2 Logging into Steam

  1. Log in to your Steam Account by either:
    • Entering your account name and password into the fields on the screen
    • Scanning the QR code with the Steam App on your phone

Note:

  • If you do not have a Steam Account, please visit: https://store.steampowered.com/join to create an account or click on the "Create Account" button.

4 Games Library

4.1 Downloading Games

  1. Click the "Steam" button
  2. Go to "library"
  3. Select the game you wish to download
  4. Select "Install"

    5. Note:

    • If you have not purchased any games yet, skip to Section 5, "The Steam Store", for how to purchase a game

4.2 Launching Games

  1. Once you have downloaded and installed games, they will show up in the "Library" menu
  2. To play a game, find it in the "Library" menu
  3. Select "Play" to begin playing the game

4.3 Game Overlay

Quick Access

Selecting the "Quick Access" menu while in game allows you to do several things:
  • The "Notifications" tab allows you to view any notifications you have
  • The "Friends" tab allows you to view your Friends List activity

    • To learn more about the Friendslist, see Section 6, "Friends and Chatting"
